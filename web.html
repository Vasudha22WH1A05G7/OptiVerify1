About Us

At OptiVerify, we redefine serviceability through optimal searches of pincodes and merchants. Our platform empowers merchants to define the pincodes they serve, while providing buyers with real-time verification of serviceability. With a focus on over 30K pincodes and millions of merchants, our data structure ensures near-instantaneous verification, enhancing the retail and logistics experience.
